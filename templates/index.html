Our Process

Our Process

Phase 1

Definition

We teach you how to develop your technical understanding and mobility. This is designed so that you need no prerequisite knowledge or experience to attend.

Phase 2

Implementation

We work on practical strategies for the implementation of what you have learned so far both during our live sessions and in your own time.

Phase 3

Reviewing

We use this time to recap what we have learned so far and implement deliberate practice to crystallise your understanding.

Phase 4

Combination

This is where the real benefit of the classes begin to show. We work to combine the gains in our technical knowledge with resistence training to practice a well rounded workout.
